PAYDAY FOOTBALL

Payday is a personal project inspired by my passion for sports and fantasy football. It is a game that allows you to draft and manage entire football teams as you compete in weekly matchups against other owners. It's my variation on the game that removes the headaches of traditional rules and setup. I created these lo-fi wireframes to demonstrate my understanding of content strategy and information architecture. The goal was to guide users with clear, helpful language to explain concepts and rules.

Taxonomy & Labeling

The rules are grouped and labeled so users can easily navigate to relevant sections on the page. My focus was to simplify the complex information while maintaining a high level of engagement.
